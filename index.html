Kinect hack

Develop of a project on Computer Music course and Sound Analysis, Synthesis and Processing course, which aims at control different guitar effects like Delay, Chorus and Wah-Wah using the movement of the musician’s body, tracked by a Microsoft Kinect and a rotation sensor like the one embedded in modern smartphones. We managed to control effects developed by us with SuperCollider and also others included in the H9 by Eventide, using MIDI messages.
We made also a website to show the project more in detail and to see some demos: here.

Master Thesis

Master thesis done in the ISPL (Image Sound Processing Laboratory) in DEIB at Politecnico di Milano.
The master thesis title is "Music Emotion Detection: A Framework Based on Electrodermal Activities". It focus on the idea that during the music listening, a human perceive some emotions that are conveyed by music.
We created a framework based on Machine Learning algorithms to recognize these emotions based on the audio data analysis combined with physiological data, in particular with Electrodermal Acrivities of the skin.
The thesis is accessible from the Politecnico library.
